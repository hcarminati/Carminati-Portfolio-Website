About Me

Computer Science and Design Major

Hi, my name is Heather Carminati. I love traveling and making art.

I have been lost in the world of shapes and colors for as long as I can remember.
As I grew up, drawing led to design and design led to coding. My growing passion
for design has pushed me into the professional world. I have carved out a name
for myself as a freelancer by designing websites for local beauty salons, auto body
shops, fundraising organizations, and even pottery artists that help them run their businesses smoothly. My goal is to take my passion for design and development and
use it to give back to society and spread positivity.
