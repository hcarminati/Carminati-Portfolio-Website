About Me

Hi, my name is Heather Carminati. I love traveling and making art.

I live in Massachusetts and I am currently studying Computer Science and Design at Northeastern University.

I have been lost in the world of shapes and colors for as long as I can remember. As I grew up, drawing led to design and design led to coding. My passion for web development has pushed me into the professional world. I have carved out a name for myself as a freelancer by designing and building websites for local beauty salons, auto body shops, fundraising organizations, and even pottery artists that help them run their businesses smoothly. My goal is to take my passion for design and development and use it to give back to society and spread positivity.
